## Dinners, day by day

### Monday — Coconut red-lentil dal, rice & green beans
**Vegan core:** Simmer 250 g red lentils with onion, garlic, ginger, turmeric, cumin and a tin of coconut milk until soft and creamy. Serve over basmati rice with green beans sautéed in a little oil. Finish with lemon and coriander.
- **Kids:** halved boiled eggs, or oven fish fingers.
- **User:** a spoon of plain yoghurt stirred in.
- **Toddler:** mash dal into rice, beans cut into short pieces, hold the salt.

### Tuesday — Penne with roasted zucchini–tomato sauce
**Vegan core:** Roast 2 zucchini (diced) with olive oil; stir into a simple garlic–tomato sauce with basil. Toss through penne.
- **Kids:** pan-cooked Wurst slices on the side.
- **User:** grated parmesan.
- **Toddler:** use small pasta shapes or cut the penne; sauce is naturally soft.

### Wednesday — Herby traybake: new potatoes, fennel, carrots & chickpeas
**Vegan core:** Toss new potatoes, fennel wedges, carrots and a tin of chickpeas with olive oil, rosemary and thyme. Roast ~35 min until golden.
- **Kids:** a baked salmon (or white fish) fillet alongside.
- **User:** yoghurt mixed with dill and a little lemon as a dip.
- **Toddler:** soft potato and carrot cut small; skip the crispy edges.

### Thursday — Lentil & vegetable soup with crusty bread
**Vegan core:** Brown lentils simmered with onion, carrot, celery, tomato and a bay leaf into a hearty soup. Serve with good crusty bread.
- **Kids:** sliced Wurst dropped into their bowl.
- **User:** cheese on the bread.
- **Toddler:** blend a portion smooth, low salt, serve warm not hot.

### Friday — Spinach–chickpea curry (palak chana) with rice
**Vegan core:** Wilt fresh spinach into a tomato-onion masala (cumin, coriander, garam masala) with chickpeas. Serve with rice.
- **Kids:** scrambled egg on the side.
- **User:** pan-fried paneer cubes folded in.
- **Toddler:** mild spice, mash with rice.

### Saturday — Flatbread "pizza" night with summer veg
**Vegan core:** Flatbreads or pizza bases topped with tomato sauce, thin zucchini, peppers and olives; bake hot. Fun to let the 4-year-old help assemble.
- **Kids:** ham or tuna topping on theirs.
- **User:** mozzarella on your slice.
- **Toddler:** cut into small soft squares, go easy on toppings.

### Sunday — Coconut–cashew vegetable korma + rice
**Vegan core:** Mixed summer veg (carrot, peas, green beans, potato) simmered in a mild cashew–coconut sauce. Big batch — leftovers cover Monday lunch. Serve with rice.
- **Kids:** baked fish or a boiled egg.
- **User:** a swirl of cream or yoghurt.
- **Toddler:** mild, mash veg into rice.

## Weekend prep tip
On Sunday, make the **vegetable korma as a big batch** — it reheats well and covers Monday's lunch. While the oven is on for Wednesday's traybake, roast a little extra veg to fold into Thursday's soup or Saturday's flatbreads. Wash and prep the green beans, fennel and carrots early in the week so weeknight cooking is mostly assembly.
